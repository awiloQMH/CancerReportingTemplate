RNA-based Tests

List of RT-PCR tests available in the laboratory.

RNA is extracted from tumor-rich tissue region or from the whole FFPE sections, and is converted to cDNA. PCR is performed using specific primers from exons across different documented fusion points from the corresponding partner genes of the chimeric transcripts. The identities of the specific PCR products in agarose gel electrophoresis are confirmed by Sanger sequencing. DNA integrity is checked across PCR products of sizes 153, 247, 323 and 429 bp.
