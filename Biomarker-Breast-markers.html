From Venetis et al Front. Mol. Biosci. 2022.

In the DESTINY-Breast 04 and 06 trials, “HER2 low” was considered IHC Score 1+ or 2+ / ISH negative, and “HER2 ultralow” was HER2 IHC Score of 0 (pattern 0+) with membrane staining that is incomplete and faint / barely perceptible in less than or equal to 10% of tumor cells. Breast cancers with these staining patterns may be eligible for treatment with trastuzumab-deruxtecan in the metastatic setting (but those with no staining, IHC 0, are currently excluded)

HER2-low
HER2 IHC 2+ AND HER2 DISH negative OR HER2 IHC 1+
Tarantino P et al. 2020

HER2 Ultra-low
It is immunohistochemically characterized by faint/barely perceptible and incomplete staining in =<10% of tumor cells without amplification on FISH.
